about=Implements the Red Relief Image Map (RRIM) method proposed by Chiba, Kaneta & Suzuki (2008) for high-detail terrain visualization and geomorphological interpretation. The plugin provides a RRIM Generator for slope and differential openness layers, plus a separate RRIM RGB Composer for RGB composition workflows, optimized for high-resolution DEMs, particularly LiDAR-derived datasets. The RRIM Generator requires the RVT plugin because differential openness is computed with RVT tools.
